Welcome to Felis-Catus-R-Us!

Felis-Catus-R-Us is a website to serve as am imformational resource for all who would like to learn more about the domestic cat. Our goal is to put the various misconceptions about the domestic cat to rest, teach you the do's and don'ts for when there is a cat around, and help people understand their furry little friend just a little bit more. The information we provide is useful even for those who don't own a cat.
