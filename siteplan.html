Site name will be Dry Oar River Adventures and the domain name will be dryoarriveradventures.com
This site would be for college students of (18 years or older) who want to break up their monotonous routine with an adventurous experience.

This site will allow potential customers to do the following:
  • Learn about various plans they can choose from for their next rafting adventure.
  • Learn about pricing options and what plan works best for them.
  • Learn about the legal safety requirements.
  • Book a specified time and date for their adventure.
