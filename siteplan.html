Site name will be Dry Oar Rafting and the proposed domain name is dryoarrafting.com
The purpose of this site is to inform potential customers of the whitewater rafting options we have available, allow them to book or cancel trips, and to provide information on all necessary safety measures.
The target audience for this site would be for groups of college students of 18 years or older who want to break up their routine with an adventurous experience.
